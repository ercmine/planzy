# Competition

Competition is the main place to **earn DRYAD** across missions, discovery, leaderboards, and claimable reward pools.

## How to earn DRYAD

- Complete daily, weekly, and seasonal missions.
- Rank on city, global, discovery, quality, and tipping leaderboards.
- Unlock place discovery bonuses for first reviews, first-five reviews, and under-covered places.
- Build streaks for repeat contribution bonuses.
- Claim competition rewards after missions or leaderboard periods settle.

## 48-hour quality model

Dryad quality is driven by **valid likes in the first 48 hours after publish**.

- `published_at` starts the quality window.
- `quality_window_ends_at = published_at + 48 hours`.
- `early_like_count` only counts deduped, valid likes during that window.
- Likes after the 48-hour window do **not** change competition quality.

### Default quality bands

- 0–4 likes → `LOW`
- 5–14 likes → `STANDARD`
- 15–39 likes → `HIGH`
- 40+ likes → `FEATURED`

## Anti-abuse rules

Competition scoring is backend-authoritative.

- One valid like per user per video.
- Banned, blocked, or fraud-flagged likes do not count.
- Removed or blocked videos stop contributing to active competition rewards.
- Duplicate reward issuance is prevented with deterministic reward source IDs.

## Claiming rewards

Competition rewards become claimable in the same DRYAD claim flow as other creator rewards.

- Mission rewards are fixed payouts.
- Leaderboard rewards use pooled DRYAD distributions.
- Claims are recorded with deterministic transaction signatures in the backend reward ledger.
