# FAQ

## Does Dryad require analytics?

No. Analytics are disabled by default and controlled via environment variables.

## Can I self-host documentation?

Yes. The docs site is static and can be deployed to Vercel, Netlify, Cloudflare Pages, or any static host.

## Where can I report issues?

Use the support email listed on the Support page.


## DRYAD economy

DRYAD place rewards are assigned per place, not globally. The first approved review at a place earns the highest reward and later approved reviews step down through the ladder. Duplicate, spam, rejected, or policy-blocked reviews do not advance the ladder.

Creators claim approved rewards by linking a Phantom-compatible Solana wallet and signing a one-time nonce challenge. Nonces expire and are single-use to prevent replay.

Video tips use SPL-token-native accounting with gross, fee, and net amounts tracked separately. Deleted, blocked, or non-eligible videos cannot receive DRYAD tips.
